Privacy Policy

Brainwave Viewer is designed to display information from compatible EEG headbands while minimizing the collection and transmission of user data.

Information processed by the app

Brainwave Viewer may process the following information locally on your device:

  • Bluetooth device identifiers
  • Raw EEG samples
  • Signal-quality values
  • EEG band-power values
  • Attention and meditation values when transmitted by the connected headband
  • Blink-related values
  • Headband battery level

Bluetooth access

Bluetooth access is required to discover, connect to, and receive information from compatible nearby EEG headbands. Bluetooth information is used only to provide these features.

Accounts

Brainwave Viewer does not require users to create an account.

Data storage

Brainwave Viewer does not permanently store raw EEG or headband-session information after the app session ends.

Data transmission

Brainwave Viewer does not upload EEG information, headband information, or Bluetooth device identifiers to an external server.

Brainwave Viewer does not sell personal information.

Analytics, advertising, and tracking

Brainwave Viewer does not contain third-party advertising and does not use third-party analytics or cross-app tracking services.

Children's privacy

Brainwave Viewer does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require accounts and does not transmit EEG information to the developer.

Research and educational use

Brainwave Viewer is intended for research, educational, and development purposes only. It is not a medical device and must not be used for diagnosis, treatment, prevention, medical monitoring, or medical decision-making.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app's features or data practices change. The updated policy will be posted on this website with a new effective date.
