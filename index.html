EEG research and education

View live brainwave data clearly.

Brainwave Viewer connects to compatible WAVE-1 EEG headbands and displays live raw EEG signals, signal quality, brainwave bands, and available NeuroSky metrics.

View Support Privacy Policy

Features

Live EEG waveform

View incoming raw EEG samples in a live, continuously updating graph.

Signal quality

Check the headband's signal-quality value and identify poor sensor contact.

Brainwave bands

View available delta, theta, alpha, beta, and gamma band-power values.

NeuroSky metrics

Display available attention, meditation, and blink-related information.

Automatic connection

Discover nearby compatible headbands using Bluetooth Low Energy.

Local processing

Headband information is processed locally without requiring an account.

For research and education

Brainwave Viewer is intended for research, educational, and development purposes. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, prevent, or monitor any medical condition.

A compatible WAVE-1 EEG headband and a supported Apple device with Bluetooth are required.
