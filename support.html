Help and troubleshooting

Brainwave Viewer Support

Getting started

  1. Turn on your compatible WAVE-1 EEG headband.
  2. Enable Bluetooth on your Apple device.
  3. Open Brainwave Viewer.
  4. Wait for the headband card to appear.
  5. Tap the card to connect and view live data.

The headband does not appear

  • Confirm that the headband is turned on.
  • Keep the headband close to your device.
  • Confirm that Bluetooth is enabled.
  • Make sure the headband is not connected to another app or device.
  • Turn the headband off and back on.
  • Close and reopen Brainwave Viewer.

The connection fails

  • Return to the headband list and connect again.
  • Restart the headband.
  • Restart Bluetooth on your device.
  • Restart Brainwave Viewer.
  • Confirm that the headband has sufficient battery.

Signal quality is poor

Signal quality can be affected by headband placement, movement, hair, or inconsistent sensor contact.

Adjust the headband so its sensors maintain consistent contact and remain still while testing.

Some metrics show “Waiting”

Available metrics depend on the information transmitted by the connected headband. Raw EEG can work even when attention, meditation, blink, or brainwave-band information is unavailable.
